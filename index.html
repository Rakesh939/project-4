YS JAGAN MOHAN REDDY

Yeduguri Sandinti Jagan Mohan Reddy (born 21 December 1972),also known as Y.S. jagan or mononymously jagan. Is an Indian politician and businessman serving as 17th and current Chiefminister of Andhrapradesh since 30 May 2019. He is founder and president of the Indian political party,YSR Congress party(YSRCP).He is also the son of Y.S. Rajasekhara Reddy,former chief minister of Andhrapradesh.
